ALINDAHAW BEACH RESORT

Alindahaw Beach Resort in Tukuran, Zamboanga del Sur is a hidden coastal gem perfect for a relaxing escape. Just a short drive from Pagadian City, it offers serene beaches, calm waters, and a laid-back vibe—ideal for unwinding and making memories by the shore.

Facilities

Cottages

₱300 for 10 persons and ₱500 for 20 persons. No hidden charges.

Parking Area

Spacious parking for all visitors, close to beach access.

Entrance Area

No entrance fee is required. Guests are welcome from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Beachfront & Amenities

Restrooms, grilling area, mini-store, piso WiFi, salbabida, emergency info tarpaulin, videoke, and a small security hut.
